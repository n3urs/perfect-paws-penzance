PERFECT PAWS DOG GROOMING (PENZANCE) — DEMO WEBSITE
====================================================

This is a DEMO site built to show Perfect Paws what their website could look like.

WHAT'S HERE
-----------
  index.html  -> the website page
  styles.css  -> the colours and styling (forest green + terracotta)
  images/     -> put photos and the logo in here

To preview it: double-click index.html — it opens in your browser.


BEFORE SENDING THE DEMO — RECOMMENDED
-------------------------------------
1. PHOTOS: the gallery and About photo are placeholders right now.
   Drop 4-6 of Perfect Paws' grooming photos into the "images" folder
   and the site will look far more finished. (See "Adding photos" below.)
2. LOGO: save their paw-and-scissors logo as images/logo.png.
3. REVIEWS: Helen Foster's review is real. The other two are clearly
   marked "placeholder" — swap them for real Facebook reviews, or remove.


ADDING PHOTOS
-------------
1. Save photos into the "images" folder.
2. In index.html, find a gallery box like:
     <div class="img-placeholder" data-label="groom-1"><span>Add photo</span></div>
   Replace with:
     <div class="img-placeholder"><img src="images/YOUR-PHOTO.jpg" alt="Dog after grooming"></div>
