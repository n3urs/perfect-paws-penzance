About

Hi, I'm Kate — your Penzance dog groomer

Perfect Paws is a friendly, professional grooming service based at The Old School House in Heamoor, Penzance. I genuinely adore dogs, and I take the time to make every visit calm and comfortable — especially for nervous or anxious pups who need a little extra patience.

I'm City & Guilds Level 2 and Level 3 qualified and fully insured, so your dog is in safe, capable hands. Whatever their breed, coat or temperament, I'll give them a practical, great-looking groom tailored to suit them.

  • Kind, patient handling for nervous dogs
  • Practical, breed-appropriate cuts that look great
  • Flexible appointments to fit around you

Services

Grooming tailored to your dog

Every dog is different. Message me and I'll recommend the right option for your dog's breed, coat and comfort.

🛁

Full Groom

Bath, blow-dry, brush-out, nails, ears and a breed-appropriate trim or style — the complete works.

✂️

Breed Styling & Trims

City & Guilds qualified styling and hand-finishing — practical, comfortable cuts that still look smart.

🧼

Bath & Tidy

A wash, dry and light tidy of face, feet and sanitary areas to keep your dog fresh between full grooms.

🐶

Puppy & Nervous Dogs

Gentle, reassuring sessions to build confidence — perfect for first grooms and anxious dogs.
